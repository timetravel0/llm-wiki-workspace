# Workspace Raw

This folder is the workspace-level intake area for source material that has not yet been assigned to a specific wiki.

## Use This Folder For

- new source material waiting to be classified
- source packs for a new wiki that has not been created yet
- material that may become its own wiki after triage

## Workflow

1. Deposit the material here first.
2. Ask for a new wiki when the material should become its own knowledge base.
3. I will triage the material, choose or propose a wiki slug, bootstrap the wiki, and move the source into `wikis/<wiki-slug>/raw/`.
4. Ingest it inside that wiki.
